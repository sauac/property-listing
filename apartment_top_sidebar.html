This well presented two-bedroom detached bungalow is situated in close proximity to Blackheath Station which operates National Rail services into London in under thirty minutes. Ideally located within close proximity to St Mathew's School.

The property comprises of a spacious reception room featuring a fireplace which flows effortlessly into the dining room with patio doors leading onto the rear garden. The kitchen is fitted with wall and base units, run of worktops and space for appliances. Completing this property are two double bedrooms benefiting from fitted wardrobes, bedroom three and a family shower room.

To the rear of the property there is a large patio area ideal for entertaining in the summer months and the rest of the garden is laid to lawn and boasts a wooden storage shed. To the front of the property there is ample off-street parking with access to a detached garage.
